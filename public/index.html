Survival IO Game

Survival IO is a multiplayer browser game where you gather resources, craft tools, and battle mobs and other players to survive as long as possible. Explore the world, level up, and become the top survivor!

How to Play

  • Use your mouse and keyboard to move, gather, and fight.
  • Collect resources like wood, stone, and food.
  • Craft tools and weapons to improve your chances.
  • Watch your health and hunger bars!
  • Compete with other players for survival and XP.
